Sinag Sports

FEU Roosevelt Inter-Class Sports Competition

About the Competition

Sinag Sports is FEU Roosevelt Marikina's annual inter-class sports competition where students from different tribe compete in various athletic events. This tournament showcases the incredible talent, teamwork, and sportsmanship of our Tamaraw athletes.

From basketball to volleyball, badminton to chess, students battle it out with pride for their respective classes, creating unforgettable moments of victory and camaraderie.

Featured Sports

Women's Basketball

Experience the energy and teamwork of our women’s basketball players as they showcase skill, strategy, and sportsmanship on the court.

Men's Basketball

Watch the excitement unfold as our men’s basketball teams compete with passion, precision, and unstoppable drive to win.

Women's Volleyball

A display of strength, coordination, and teamwork. Women’s volleyball players bring every match to life with powerful spikes and quick reflexes.

Men's Volleyball

Feel the intensity as the men’s volleyball teams deliver thrilling rallies, strong serves, and unmatched determination in every set.

Badminton

Fast-paced and exciting, badminton combines agility, accuracy, and endurance for a truly entertaining match.

Table Tennis

A fast-paced and exciting game of precision and quick reflexes. Players showcase their agility and control in thrilling rallies that test focus, strategy, and skill.

Chess

A classic game of intellect and strategy where players challenge each other in thoughtful moves and smart plays.

Scrabble

Words meet competition as participants showcase creativity and vocabulary skills in this fun and challenging word game.

Dance Competition

A fun-filled event featuring creative dance performances, with style, energy, and imagination.
